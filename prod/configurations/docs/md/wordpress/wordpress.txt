##How to get Twitter Consumer Key & Consumer Secret?

### Step 1
Go to https://developer.wordpress.com/.
Click on the **My Apps**

![](//user-account.lrcontent.com/prod/configurations/docs/md/wordpress/images/step1.png)


### Step 2
Log in to your Wordpress Account if not already logged in.

![](//user-account.lrcontent.com/prod/configurations/docs/md/wordpress/images/step2.png)

### Step 3
Click on the **Create New Application**

![](//user-account.lrcontent.com/prod/configurations/docs/md/wordpress/images/step3.png)

### Step 4

Enter the Application Name and Description. Enter <br> <span class="hidden-input copyKey" readonly >**#protocol#://#sitename#**</span> in Website URL field and <br><span class="hidden-input copyKey" readonly >**#protocol#://#sitename##port#/socialauth/validate.sauth**</span> in Redirect URL field. Select Web in type and Fill the Security Question and hit Create Button.

![](//user-account.lrcontent.com/prod/configurations/docs/md/wordpress/images/step4.png)


### Step 5
Go to **My Application** and Click on your Wordpress Application.

![](//user-account.lrcontent.com/prod/configurations/docs/md/wordpress/images/step5.png)

### Step 6
Copy and paste the **Client ID** and **Secret** respectively to "WordPress Client ID and WordPress Client Secret" fields on the left-hand side box.

![](//user-account.lrcontent.com/prod/configurations/docs/md/wordpress/images/step6.png)
