##How to get Pinterest App ID & App Secret?

###Step 1
Go to [https://developers.pinterest.com/apps/](https://developers.pinterest.com/apps/) and login with your Pinterest account. Accept the terms conditions and click on the **Create app** button

![](//user-account.lrcontent.com/prod/configurations/docs/md/pinterest/images/step-1.png)

###Step 2
Enter the Name and Description for the app, and click on the **Create** button

![](//user-account.lrcontent.com/prod/configurations/docs/md/pinterest/images/step-2.png)

###Step 3
Scroll down to the App detail page, Enter the following in **Site URL**: <span class="hidden-input copyKey" readonly >#protocol#://#sitename#</span> <br>
Now enter the following in **Redirect URIs**:
<span class="hidden-input copyKey" readonly >#protocol#://#sitename#:443/socialauth/validate.sauth</span>.<br> Save the changes.

![](//user-account.lrcontent.com/prod/configurations/docs/md/pinterest/images/step-3.png)

###Step 4
Scroll up to the App detail page, Click on the **Show** button to get the App Secret.

![](//user-account.lrcontent.com/prod/configurations/docs/md/pinterest/images/step-4.png)

###Step 5

Copy the App ID and App Secret into the corresponding fields in this app configuration guide.

![](//user-account.lrcontent.com/prod/configurations/docs/md/pinterest/images/step-5.png)
