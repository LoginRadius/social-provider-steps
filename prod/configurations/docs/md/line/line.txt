## How to get Line Channel ID & Channel Secret?

### Step 1
Go to the [channel console](https://developers.line.biz/en/?status=success) and click on **log in to console** and login into your business account.

![](//user-account.lrcontent.com/prod/configurations/docs/md/line/images/step1.png)


### Step 2
Select **create new provider**, enter provider name and click on **create**.

![](//user-account.lrcontent.com/prod/configurations/docs/md/line/images/step2.png)

### Step 3
Under **Channels**, click on **Create a LINE Login Channel**.

![](//user-account.lrcontent.com/prod/configurations/docs/md/line/images/step3.png)


### Step 4
Enter all the required fields.

![](//user-account.lrcontent.com/prod/configurations/docs/md/line/images/step4.png)


### Step 5
Select **App types** as **Web app**.

![](//user-account.lrcontent.com/prod/configurations/docs/md/line/images/step5.png)

### Step 6 
After entering all the fields check the **LINE Developers Agreement** and click on **Create**.

![](//user-account.lrcontent.com/prod/configurations/docs/md/line/images/step6.png)

### Step 7 
Navigate to the **LINE Login** tab and in the **Callback URL**, enter <span class="hidden-input copyKey" readonly >**#protocol#://#sitename#:443/socialauth/validate.sauth**</span> and click on **Update**.

![](//user-account.lrcontent.com/prod/configurations/docs/md/line/images/step7.png)

### Step 8 
Click on **Developing** as shown in the below screen and select **Publish**.

![](//user-account.lrcontent.com/prod/configurations/docs/md/line/images/step8.png)

### Step 9
Navigate to the **Basic Settings** tab and Copy **Channel ID**. You can find the **Channel Secret** under same settings and paste these credentials into the corresponding fields on the left-hand side of this guide. 

![](//user-account.lrcontent.com/prod/configurations/docs/md/line/images/step9.png)
