## How to get FourSquare Client ID & Client Secret?

### Step 1

Go to the [Developers](https://developer.foursquare.com/) section of Foursquare and click on **Sign Up / Log In** at the top-right of the interface.

![](https://user-account.lrcontent.com/prod/configurations/docs/md/foursquare/images/step1.png)

### Step 2

Now click on **Log In** option under **Places API/Developer Console**.

![](https://user-account.lrcontent.com/prod/configurations/docs/md/foursquare/images/step2.png)

### Step 3

Log In to Foursquare.

![](https://user-account.lrcontent.com/prod/configurations/docs/md/foursquare/images/step3.png)

### Step 4
Click on **Create New Project**. 

![](https://user-account.lrcontent.com/prod/configurations/docs/md/foursquare/images/step4.png)

### Step 5
Enter the Project Name, then click on the **Create** button.

![](https://user-account.lrcontent.com/prod/configurations/docs/md/foursquare/images/step5.png)

### Step 6
Enter the <br><span class="hidden-input copyKey" readonly >**#protocol#://#sitename#**</span> in the **Project URL** and <br><span class="hidden-input copyKey" readonly >**#protocol#://#sitename##port#/socialauth/validate.sauth**</span> in **Redirect URL** and then add the **Privacy Policy URL** followed by clicking on the **Save Changes** button.

![](https://user-account.lrcontent.com/prod/configurations/docs/md/foursquare/images/step6.png)

### Step 7
Copy the generated **Client ID** and **Client Secret** for this app and paste them into the corresponding fields on the left-hand side of this configuration guide.

![](https://user-account.lrcontent.com/prod/configurations/docs/md/foursquare/images/step7.png)
