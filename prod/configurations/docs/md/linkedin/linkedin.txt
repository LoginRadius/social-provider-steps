### Step 1
Go to the [Developers](http://developer.linkedin.com/) section of LinkedIn. Click on the **Sign In**.

![](https://user-account.lrcontent.com/prod/configurations/docs/md/linkedin/images/step1.png)

### Step 2
Enter your login credentials and click **Sign In**.

![](https://user-account.lrcontent.com/prod/configurations/docs/md/linkedin/images/step2.png)

### Step 3
After logging in, click on **Create App**.

![](https://user-account.lrcontent.com/prod/configurations/docs/md/linkedin/images/step3.png)

### Step 4
Fill out all the required fields. After that, click on **Create App** button.

![](https://user-account.lrcontent.com/prod/configurations/docs/md/linkedin/images/step4.png)



### Step 5
 Navigate to the **Auth** tab and enter <span class="hidden-input copyKey" readonly >**#protocol#://#sitename##port#/socialauth/validate.sauth**</span> under **Authorized redirect URLs for your app**.

![](https://user-account.lrcontent.com/prod/configurations/docs/md/linkedin/images/step5.png)


### Step 6
Navigate to the **Products** tab and **Select** Sign In with LinkedIn.

![](https://user-account.lrcontent.com/prod/configurations/docs/md/linkedin/images/step6.png)

### Step 7
Navigate back to the **Auth** tab, enter **Client ID** and **Client secret** into the corresponding fields on the left-hand side of this app configuration guide.

![](https://user-account.lrcontent.com/prod/configurations/docs/md/linkedin/images/step7.png)
