##How to get SinaWeibo App Key & App Secret?

###Step 1
Go to [http://open.weibo.com/development](http://open.weibo.com/development) then click on highlighted link and Login. 

![](//user-account.lrcontent.com/prod/configurations/docs/md/sinaweibo/images/step1.png)

###Step 2
Click on the Highlighted button to create a new app. 

![](//user-account.lrcontent.com/prod/configurations/docs/md/sinaweibo/images/step2.png)

###Step 3
Select the Application type as per the highlighted in box

![](//user-account.lrcontent.com/prod/configurations/docs/md/sinaweibo/images/step3.png)

###Step 4
Fill Application Name and select Category of the application. After that hit **Creating** button.

![](//user-account.lrcontent.com/prod/configurations/docs/md/sinaweibo/images/step4.png)

###Step 5
Enter <span class="hidden-input copyKey" readonly >**#protocol#://#sitename#**</span> in Application address and add the Sina Weibo generated **App Key** and **App Secret** to LoginRadius and fill out other required fields.

![](//user-account.lrcontent.com/prod/configurations/docs/md/sinaweibo/images/step5.png)

###Step 6
Click on the highlighted button to Save the settings

![](//user-account.lrcontent.com/prod/configurations/docs/md/sinaweibo/images/step6.png)

###Step 7
Click on the highlighted link

![](//user-account.lrcontent.com/prod/configurations/docs/md/sinaweibo/images/step7.png)

###Step 8
Click on the highlighted link to edit the details

![](//user-account.lrcontent.com/prod/configurations/docs/md/sinaweibo/images/step8.png)

###Step 9
Enter <span class="hidden-input copyKey" readonly >**#protocol#://#sitename##port#/socialauth/validate.sauth**</span> into both text boxes. After that click on highlighted button to save settings

![](//user-account.lrcontent.com/prod/configurations/docs/md/sinaweibo/images/step9.png)
