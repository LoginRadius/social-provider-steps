## How to get Paypal Client ID & Client Secret?

### Step 1
Go to [Developer Portal](https://developer.paypal.com/developer/applications) and login with your developer Paypal Account<br>Click on the **Create Application** button.

![](//user-account.lrcontent.com/prod/configurations/docs/md/paypal/images/step1.png)

### Step 2
Under **My Apps and Credentials**, click on **Create App**

![](//user-account.lrcontent.com/prod/configurations/docs/md/paypal/images/step2.png)

### Step 3
Input **App Name** and click on **Create App**.

![](//user-account.lrcontent.com/prod/configurations/docs/md/paypal/images/step3.png)

### Step 4
Click on **Show** under API Credentials. Copy and paste the Client ID and Secret into the corresponding fields on the left hand side of this app configuration guide.  

![](//user-account.lrcontent.com/prod/configurations/docs/md/paypal/images/step4.png)

### Step 5
Under App Settings, click on **Show** for the Return URL. In the **Return URL** field, enter the following:<br> <span class="hidden-input copyKey" readonly >**#protocol#://#sitename##port#/socialauth/validate.sauth**</span>. Click the **+** button.

![](//user-account.lrcontent.com/prod/configurations/docs/md/paypal/images/step5.png)

### Step 6
Select **Log in with PayPal** and cick on **Advanced options** as shown.

![](//user-account.lrcontent.com/prod/configurations/docs/md/paypal/images/step6.png)

### Step 7
Select the options you would like to enable as per your requirement and click on **save** button.

![](//user-account.lrcontent.com/prod/configurations/docs/md/paypal/images/step7.png)
