##How to get Twitter Consumer Key & Consumer Secret?

###Step 1
Go to the [Twitter Developers](https://developer.twitter.com/en) section and log in with your Twitter credentials.

![](//user-account.lrcontent.com/prod/configurations/docs/md/twitter/images/step-1.png)


###Step 2
Click on **Add App**.

![](//user-account.lrcontent.com/prod/configurations/docs/md/twitter/images/step-2.png)

###Step 3
Name your Twitter app and click **Next**.

![](//user-account.lrcontent.com/prod/configurations/docs/md/twitter/images/step-3.png)

###Step 4
Keep the displayed **API Key** and **API Secret** to be used in the last step and click on **App settings**.

![](//user-account.lrcontent.com/prod/configurations/docs/md/twitter/images/step-4.png)

###Step 5
Click on **Set up** under **User authentication settings**.

![](//user-account.lrcontent.com/prod/configurations/docs/md/twitter/images/step-5.png)

###Step 6
Select **Read** under **App permissions** and enable the **Request email from users** options. Under **Callback URI / Redirect URL**, enter: <br><span class="hidden-input copyKey" readonly >**#protocol#://#sitename##port#/socialauth/validate.sauth**</span>, and under **Website URL**, enter:<br> <span class="hidden-input copyKey" readonly >**#protocol#://#sitename#**</span>. Fill out all the required fields and click on the **Save** button. 

![](//user-account.lrcontent.com/prod/configurations/docs/md/twitter/images/step-6.png)

###Step 7
Use the **API Key** and **API Secret** you saved in **Step 4** for your Twitter app configuration.
