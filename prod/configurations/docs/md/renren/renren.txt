##How to get Renren API Key & Secret Key?

###Step 1
Go to [http://dev.renren.com/app](http://dev.renren.com/app) .<br>Click on the button located in the top right corner (as shown in the image below).

![](//user-account.lrcontent.com/prod/configurations/docs/md/renren/images/step1.png)

###Step 2
Login to your Renren account.

![](//user-account.lrcontent.com/prod/configurations/docs/md/renren/images/step2.png)

###Step 3
In both drop-down menus, please select the second option from the bottom. <br>Enter your description and <span class="hidden-input copyKey" readonly >**#sitename#**</span> in root domain.

![](//user-account.lrcontent.com/prod/configurations/docs/md/renren/images/step3.png)

###Step 4
Upload your 16x16, 48x48, 75x75 and 100x100 icons and click the **save** button.

![](//user-account.lrcontent.com/prod/configurations/docs/md/renren/images/step4.png)

###Step 5
Renren will generate your API Key and Secret, which you will need to copy and paste these into the relevant fields in this LoginRadius app configuration guide.

![](//user-account.lrcontent.com/prod/configurations/docs/md/renren/images/step5.png)

###Step 6
Click on the “+” icon.

![](//user-account.lrcontent.com/prod/configurations/docs/md/renren/images/step6.png)

###Step 7
Enter valid Canvas Page Name and Copy the "Canvas URL" into "Wap 应用访问URL " and <span class="hidden-input copyKey" readonly >**#protocol#://#sitename##port#/socialauth/validate.sauth**</span> into callback address and click **save** button.

![](//user-account.lrcontent.com/prod/configurations/docs/md/renren/images/step7.png)
