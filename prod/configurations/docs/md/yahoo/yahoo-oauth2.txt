##How to get Yahoo Consumer Key & Consumer Secret?

###Step1

Go to [Developer](http://developer.yahoo.com/) section of Yahoo. CLick on **My Apps** menu and select **YDN Apps** and you’ll be required to log in.

![](//account.lrcontent.com/dev/docs/md/yahoo/images/step1.png)

###Step2

After logging in, accept Yahoo’s terms of use(if it is a new dev account). Then, click on **"Create an App"**.

![](//account.lrcontent.com/dev/docs/md/yahoo/images/step2.png)

###Step3

Enter the App Name and other details. Select **"Web Application"** under Application Type menu and include for "Home Page URL" and "Callback Domain" the following domain <span class='highlighted-text'>https://impl.hub.loginradius.com</span>

![](//account.lrcontent.com/dev/docs/md/yahoo/images/step3.png)

###Step4

Under the **API Permissions** sectioon select **Profile** and under **Profile** section select **Read Public** permission. 

After setting permissions, click **Create App**.

![](//account.lrcontent.com/dev/docs/md/yahoo/images/step4.png)

###Step5

Yahoo will generate Consumer Key and Secret for your App. Now copy and add Consumer Key and Secret to your LoginRadius account.

**Note** :Any changes made to the app will generate a new Consumer Key.

![](//account.lrcontent.com/dev/docs/md/yahoo/images/step5.png)

###Step6
1. Verify your domain <span class='highlighted-text'>**impl.hub.loginradius.com**</span>. To do so, go to https://developer.apps.yahoo.com/manage and hit **Verify** button next to your app.
2. This will open up a pop-up, in which you get a Yahoo verification **File Name** in point-1, copy this file name and paste to the box "Yahoo Verification File Name" on the left side here and Hit **Save** button
3. Hit **Verify Domain** button and close the pop-up after verification.
----------
