##How to get Facebook App ID & App Secret?

###Step1
Go to [Facebook Developer](https://developers.facebook.com/) link and log in with your facebook credentials.

![](https://cdn.loginradius.com/ua/v12/images/HelpStepImage/v2-fb-01v1.png)

**NOTE**: Please do not use business account because you can not create facebook app using business account.

###Step 2
Click on **Apps** and Choose **Create a New App** option.

![](https://cdn.loginradius.com/ua/v12/images/HelpStepImage/v2-fb-02v1.png)

###Step 3
Select website platform.

![](https://cdn.loginradius.com/ua/v12/images/HelpStepImage/v3-fb-03.png)

###Step4
Click on Skip and Create App Id

![](https://cdn.loginradius.com/ua/v12/images/HelpStepImage/v3-fb-04.png)


###Step5
A pop-up box will appear, enter **Display Name** and Select **Category** for app and hit **Create App** button.
**Tip**: Choose 'Display Name' as name of your website or your company name.

![](https://cdn.loginradius.com/ua/v12/images/HelpStepImage/v3-fb-05.png)

###Step 6
Select **Settings** menu from left sidebar then Click on **+Add Platform**.

![](https://cdn.loginradius.com/ua/v12/images/HelpStepImage/v3-fb-06.png)


###Step 7
Select **website** platform.

![](https://cdn.loginradius.com/ua/v12/images/HelpStepImage/v3-fb-07.png)

###Step 8
Enter this in **App Domains** : impl.hub.loginradius.com
Enter this in**Site URL** : https://impl.hub.loginradius.com
after that click on **Save Changes** button.


###Step 9
Select **Status & Review** menu at left sidebar and change App status as highlighted.

![](https://cdn.loginradius.com/ua/v12/images/HelpStepImage/v3-fb-09.png)


###Step 10
A pop-up box will appear for confirmation. Hit Confirm button in the popup.

![](https://cdn.loginradius.com/ua/v12/images/HelpStepImage/v3-fb-10.png)


###Step 11
Select **Dashboard** menu from left sidebar. Add **API Key** and **Secret Key** to LoginRadius.

![](https://cdn.loginradius.com/ua/v12/images/HelpStepImage/v3-fb-11.png)

###Step 12
You need to get your Facebook app approved to get access to data and features beyond the public profile.

In order to configure your Facebook app to be successfully approved, refer support document [here](http://support.loginradius.com/hc/en-us/articles/200912879-How-do-I-submit-my-Facebook-app-for-review)
